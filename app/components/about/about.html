This app was created and developed my John Marlett. for more about me, or to get in touch, visit my website at classicalmarlett.org

If you are classisict and have concerns about the accuracy of this Odyssey because of chronological or thematic conflicts, first, I applaud you. Secondly, my riposte will be Levi Strauss's Structuralist argument of mythical constituents. The central logic of this app assumes the Structuralist view whether I personally condone it or not.
